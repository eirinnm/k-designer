Option 1: Provide a list of SNPs with chromosome, position, reference base and mutant base.

A name can be provided (optional). The appropriate sequence will be taken from danRer11.

Option 2: Provide a sequence with the mutation in the form [WM], where W is wildtype base and M is mutant.

Include 50bp on either side of the mutation.

For deletions, use the shortest common sequence and make M the first base after the deletion.
